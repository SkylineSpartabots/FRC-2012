enum Ports::DigitalSidecar::Gpio

Used frequently for sensors. When used with a jumper, can also be used to power servos.

Stands for 'General Purpose Input/Output'.

Known uses:

  • Limit switches
  • Servos
  • Accelerometer/Gyro (?)
