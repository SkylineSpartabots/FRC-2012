Provides a thin layer to control the elevator using a joystick.

Warning:
Inaccurate details.

Button 8 causes the conveyor belt to move the ball up until it reaches the top. The driver must press button 9 to continue moving the conveyor belt, i.e. load the ball into the shooter.

A limit switch at the middle of the conveyor belt tells the driver if there is a ball at the middle of the conveyor belt; this way, the driver has room to decide to pick up another ball or continue moving the belt (and shoot the ball).
